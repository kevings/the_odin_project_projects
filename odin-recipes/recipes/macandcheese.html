Mac and Cheese

Description

My go-to lazy dinner

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Nutritional yeast
  • Plant-based milk of your choosing
  • Plant-based butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Field Roast Apple and Sage Sausages
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Boil water, then add pasta
  2. Cut up the sausage while that's happening
  3. Drain the pasta, put it back in the pot
  4. Add milk, butter, nutritional yeast and stir it around
  5. Add sausages, spinach, salt and pepper to taste
  6. Mix it around until spinach is desired wilt-level and serve
